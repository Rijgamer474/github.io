About

Hello! My name is Rijan Shah, and I’m from the beautiful country of Nepal. I’m a proud Hindu, and my diverse interests and passions have shaped both my personal and professional journey.

One of my major passions is video editing. I’ve always been fascinated by the creative process of transforming raw footage into something visually engaging and meaningful. Although I would consider myself intermediate in video editing, I’m constantly learning and honing my skills to improve. The art of storytelling through visuals has always intrigued me, and I find great satisfaction in experimenting with different styles and techniques to bring ideas to life.

In addition to video editing, I’m currently diving into the world of programming. I’m learning Python, a language that I believe is foundational for a future career in data science. My ultimate goal is to become a data scientist, a field that excites me due to its potential for solving real-world problems and making data-driven decisions. I find the process of analyzing data, uncovering patterns, and building models to be both challenging and rewarding, and I look forward to the journey ahead.
